Child Safety Standards

Last updated: December 1, 2025

At i-Recommend, we are deeply committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users, with specific attention to the safety and well-being of minors. We have zero tolerance for content or behavior that exploits or endangers children.

1. Zero Tolerance Policy

We strictly prohibit any content that depicts, promotes, or normalizes the sexual exploitation or abuse of minors. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)
  • Grooming or predatory behavior
  • Sexualization of minors
  • Cyberbullying or harassment of minors

2. Content Moderation & Reporting

We employ a combination of automated technologies and human moderation to detect and remove harmful content. We also encourage our community to report any concerns immediately.

If you see something that violates our child safety standards, please report it directly through the app or contact us immediately.

3. Cooperation with Law Enforcement

We comply with all applicable laws regarding child safety. If we detect CSAM or evidence of imminent harm to a child, we will report it to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and appropriate law enforcement agencies.

4. Age Requirements

i-Recommend is intended for users who are at least 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account.

5. Resources for Parents & Guardians

We encourage parents and guardians to be involved in their children's online activities. We recommend discussing online safety and privacy with your children.
