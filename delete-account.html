Request Account Deletion

We respect your right to control your data. If you wish to delete your i-Recommend account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your profile information, posts, and history will be permanently removed.

How to Request Deletion

To verify your identity and process your request securely, please send an email to our support team with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".

Please include the following information in your email:

  • Your full name
  • The email address associated with your account
  • Your username (if applicable)
Send Deletion Request Email

We will process your request within 30 days and confirm via email once your account has been deleted.
